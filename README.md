# Meal Prep Pro — Conversion Lab

A collection of experiments focused on improving signup, onboarding, and paywall conversion for Meal Prep Pro.

This repo documents real decisions, tradeoffs, and learnings.

---

## Focus Areas

* Paywall variants
* Pricing communication
* Onboarding flow
* Feature gating
* Trial activation

---

## Example Experiments

### Paywall Simplification

**Hypothesis:** Reducing plan complexity increases trial starts.

Changes:

* Fewer tiers
* Stronger outcome messaging
* Social proof placement

Status: In progress

---

### Outcome-First Messaging

**Hypothesis:** Result-focused messaging outperforms feature lists.

Changes:

* Hero rewrite
* Visual meal plan examples
* CTA repositioning

Status: Planned

---

## Instrumentation

* Funnel event tracking
* Variant tagging
* Drop-off analysis

---

## Learnings (ongoing)

* Time savings messaging resonates strongly
* Overly detailed paywalls create hesitation
* Visual examples reduce friction

---

## Why share publicly

Growth work is iterative and messy.
Documenting it creates reusable knowledge.
